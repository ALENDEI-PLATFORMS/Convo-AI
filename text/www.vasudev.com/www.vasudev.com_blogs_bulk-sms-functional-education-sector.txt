 6 Best Functions Of Bulk SMS in the education sector 

Author :  Vasudev Global Digital Team
                | Date :  September 21, 2022 |
                          
		  





 

How can Bulk SMS be functional in the education sector?
If you are here, you have educational institutes you want to promote. Here we are providing some features of Bulk SMS in the educational sector. Bulk SMS in the education sector has many benefits, like the institute can send SMS to the student informing such school's crucial information. Additionally, Bulk SMS has surplus advantages which can not be underrated. This article will explain how the institute can promote its features using bulk SMS service. Besides, how can the institutes add extra stars to their services using Bulk SMS Marketing?


First of all, what is bulk SMS marketing?
Bulk SMS Marketing is a Marketing platform in which promoters can send mass SMS to their users with just a single click. After sending the SMS, the promoter will know about the SMS's status. Also, in SMS, One can add a clickable link, and the link can divert users to the website or sales page.

Now you are aware of Bulk SMS marketing. Now we will discuss the Advantages of Bulk SMS marketing.
Advantages of Bulk SMS Service.
Bulk SMS Has tremendous benefits by which businesses can attract customers at a better and more reasonable rate.

High open rates

In today's era, most people use smartphones, but the old generation still uses keypad phones. By sending WhatsApp message, only the younger generation get the information. Instead, if we send SMS, both the age get the data, and the response rate and the SMS opening rate are high.

 Better conversion rate

Concerning other marketing tools, SMS marketing's conversion rate is high. It is commonly observed that 90% of messages are opened in just 10 seconds after getting a text message.

Budget-friendly

SMS Marketing is cheaper than any other media marketing. If we compare it with social media marketing or any other media marketing, SMS marketing is low-cost and highly effective.

Greater audience reach

With the increasing smartphone usage, SMS marketing is nothing less than Goldmine. Moreover, In Bulk, SMS marketing internet connectivity is not required. Such a non-smartphone user also gets promotional SMS on their phone, so in this medium, audience reach is more significant than in the other medium.

Campaign success visibility

How would the business get to know about the SMS Marketing Performance? Companies can observe each message's performance in a Bulk SMS Campaign. Based on this, they can change their strategy and perform better in the next campaign.

Communicate in different languages

Well, India is a diverse country, and it has many rituals and languages. If businesses try to promote their product in the local market, they must convey their message in the local language. Vasudev Global allows you to send messages in English, Hindi, Gujarati, Marathi, and other languages.

How does the Educational sector get benefit from Bulk SMS Service?

The education sector is vast. If you do some creativity in that, you will find an opportunity to grow. You will stand out from your competitors. There are many ways to show creativity and give facility to the students. By doing this, your institution gets popularity in your area or city.
Below are the benefits of Bulk SMS in the educational sector.

Fastest speed: Using SMS service, school staff can send notifications of important updates and reminders in a time-sensitive manner. Message delivery hardly takes a few seconds to reach the intended recipient.
Time-saving tool: Bulk SMS is a time-saving tool as important information can easily be conveyed to a mass audience in just a single click of a button.
Advertising tool: Apart from sending updates and notices, SMS works as a profitable tool for advertising about new courses, admissions, scholarships, etc. It is an affordable advertising tool for every institution regardless of its budget.
Distributes updates in real-time: Messages related to fee submission details such as amount due, last date for fee submission, penalty for late fees, etc. can be easily conveyed via Bulk SMS service.
Best tool for emergency communication: Information related to sudden emergencies such as holidays, exam postponement, change in bus routes, etc. can be sent in a timely manner.
Reliable medium: Exam related messages such as time-table, result announcements, admit card updates, etc. can be sent to all students at once. It is a reliable method of transferring updates in such a way that it is received by every student.
